Will I be updated on how the funds are utilised?

We will be happy to keep you informed through email and update through the year on how the funds are utilised on our website.

Is there any Age limit for donating blood?

Yes.People who are above 18 years are only eligible for donating blood.

Yes.All those are clearly mentioned in our website

If I want to send money by cheque/draft, in whose name shall I make the cheque?

All donations should be made in the name of ngo management system. Please make sure you get the receipt for the same and also please give us your details (Pan no, name, address, email id, contact no) at the time of making the contribution. We need this information as a statutory requirement under the law.

Where should we go to donate blood?

After filling registration form we will mail you , according to your location and suggest nearby services.

Can i use phone pay wallet for donation?

Yes. You can directly donate from your phone pay wallet.
