MAIN MOTIVE OF AN NGO

The motive behind the start of an NGO is to be able to provide poor,deprived children and women the basic living standards such as food, shelter and education which will enable them to live a life of dignity and self-esteem. Children being provided education and life skills are able to accomplish a good livelihood for themselves and their family.

OBJECTIVES

• Create a safe home for every child with access to clean shelters, food, and clothing.
• Stop child labour in several areas and ensure children enjoy their constitutional rights of a secure childhood.
• Work with these children to uplift their creativity and enhance their self-confidence.
• Provide skills-oriented education to empower such deprived children.
• Raise awareness about child rights, care, development, and protection
